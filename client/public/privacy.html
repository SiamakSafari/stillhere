Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Our Commitment

Still Here is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect the minimum data necessary to provide our service, and we never sell or share your personal information with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following information:

  • Your name - Stored locally on your device only
  • Emergency contact information - Name and email address of your designated contact, synced to our servers to enable alerts
  • Check-in history - Stored locally on your device, optionally synced for backup
  • Mood data (optional) - If you choose to log your mood, this is stored locally
  • Location data (optional) - Only if you enable location sharing for activity mode
  • Push notification token - If you enable notifications, we store a device token to send reminders

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Send alert emails to your emergency contact if you miss check-ins
  • Send you reminder notifications (if enabled)
  • Provide the core functionality of the app

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics profiling, or any other purpose.

3. Data Storage

Local storage: Most of your data (name, check-in history, mood data, settings) is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage or native device storage.

Server storage: Only the following data is stored on our servers:

  • Your user ID (anonymous identifier)
  • Emergency contact name and email
  • Last check-in timestamp
  • Vacation mode status
  • Push notification token (if enabled)

Our servers are hosted on secure, encrypted infrastructure. We do not store any data longer than necessary to provide the service.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Your emergency contact: We send them an alert email only if you miss check-ins for the configured period
  • Service providers: We use SendGrid for email delivery, which processes email addresses to deliver alerts
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Export your data: Use the Export Data feature in Settings to download all your data
  • Delete your data: Use the Reset App feature to delete all local and server data
  • Opt out of notifications: Disable notifications in your device settings
  • Access your data: Contact us to request a copy of any server-side data

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Encrypted storage for server-side data
  • Regular security audits
  • Minimal data collection principle

7. Children's Privacy

Still Here is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

privacy@stillhere.app

Summary

Your data is yours. We only store what's necessary to send alerts to your emergency contact. No ads, no tracking, no selling your data. Ever.
